About The T-Level Course

Structure


The T-Level consists of 8 core topics but also includes an occupational specialism, Employer set project and a Work placement. The T-level is different to the generic a-levels as it includes a work placement of 450 hours, this gives you the experience of working in the environment / career area which you may want to go into. Year 12 consists of the Core sector and also the Employer set project. Year 13 consists of the Occupational specialism and the Work placement
